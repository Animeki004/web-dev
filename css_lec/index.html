Iron Man is a fictional superhero created by Marvel Comics, first appearing in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. The character is the alter ego of Tony Stark, a billionaire genius, inventor, and industrialist who builds a high-tech armored suit to save his life and later uses it to protect the world. Stark's transformation from a self-centered weapons manufacturer to a heroic figure marks one of the most iconic journeys in comic book history. Played by Robert Downey Jr. in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Iron Man became a fan-favorite and a central figure in the Avengers, known for his intelligence, wit, and sacrifice.
